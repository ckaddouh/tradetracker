Login/Register Screen: It will allow users to either log in or register an account, either with email or Google authentication. 

Trade Ideas Journal Screen (Home Page): The web app will open to the trade ideas journal, showing the performance of recent trades I have logged. It will also have a nav bar to access the other pages, such as earnings announcements, portfolio dashboard, and options visualizer. 

Earnings Announcements Screen: It will show major earnings announcements coming up and have a watchlist page to see the earnings of companies that you are specifically watching. 

Portfolio Dashboard Screen: This shows the aggregate effect of my ideas by fetching the live prices and calculating PnL and sector breakdown (how much I would make if I actually invested in all of my ideas).
